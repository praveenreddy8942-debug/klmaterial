Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

Overview

KL Material Study Hub ("we", "us") is a free educational website providing study materials for KL University CSE students. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. This service may collect:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • Approximate geographic location (country/city level)
  • Referral source (how you found the site)

This data is aggregated and anonymized. We do not track individual users or collect personal information through Analytics.

Firebase

We use Google Firebase to store metadata about materials (such as download counts and ratings). Firebase may process standard request data (IP address, user agent) as part of normal operation. No personal accounts or login data are collected.

Downloads

Study materials are hosted on GitHub and delivered via GitHub's servers or jsDelivr CDN. When you download a file, those services may log standard access data. We do not collect or store any personal download history.

Cookies & Storage

This site uses localStorage to remember your theme preference (dark/light mode). Google Analytics may set cookies for analytics purposes. No advertising cookies are used.

Your Data

We do not:

  • Require you to create an account
  • Collect names, emails, or personal information
  • Sell or share data with third parties
  • Use advertising or tracking pixels

Third-Party Services

This site relies on the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics — Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Firebase — Firebase Privacy
  • GitHub Pages — GitHub Privacy
  • jsDelivr CDN — jsDelivr Privacy
